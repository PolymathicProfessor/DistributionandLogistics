üí≠ Discussion Questions for Group Work

Question 1: Testing vs. Forecasting

Consider a company like Shein that uses minimal initial orders to test products before scaling up. What are the advantages and disadvantages of this approach compared to traditional bulk forecasting? How might this impact inventory costs, customer satisfaction, and environmental sustainability?

Question 2: Gig Economy Ethics

Many companies now use "gig economy" workers for last-mile delivery. Discuss the ethical implications of this practice. What responsibilities should companies have regarding fair compensation, worker safety, and job security for gig workers in their distribution networks?

Question 3: Distribution Strategy Selection

A manufacturer must choose between intensive, selective, and exclusive distribution for a new high-end smartwatch. Which strategy would you recommend and why? Consider factors like brand image, pricing control, customer service, and market coverage in your answer.
